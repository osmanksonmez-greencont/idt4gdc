Project Partners

Consortium

Three European partners combining sustainability expertise, hardware engineering, and AI research — united by a mission to make data centres climate-neutral.

🇬🇧 United Kingdom
Innova Integra Limited
WP1 Lead Project Leader Organisation
Contact →

Innova Integra Limited is the project leader organisation for iDT4GDC and leads Work Package 1, which establishes the entire system architecture. The company brings deep expertise in AI optimisation modules, green energy forecasting systems, and enterprise backend architecture to the project.

As the technical anchor of the consortium, Innova Integra is responsible for the production-ready backend (KER-6) and the AI optimisation components including the Energy Source Optimisation Engine (KER-1), Green Energy Forecasting Module (KER-2), and Dynamic Energy Pricing Mechanism (KER-3). Their work on system integration ensures that all components from all partners work together seamlessly.

Contributions to iDT4GDC
  • Leading WP1: System Architecture & Backend
  • Designing and implementing the SOA backend platform (KER-6)
  • Developing the DCIM digital twin integration layer
  • Building AI/ML optimisation modules (KER-1, KER-2, KER-3)
  • Overall system integration and API gateway design
  • Open-source licensing strategy and community engagement
  • ITEA review meeting coordination and project management

🇹🇷 Turkey
BitNet Bilişim
WP4 Contributor Hardware Specialist
Contact →

BitNet Bilişim is a Turkish IT infrastructure company with specialised expertise in hardware optimisation, interactive data visualisation, and efficient compute architecture. As the primary contributor to Work Package 4, BitNet brings deep technical knowledge of physical hardware behaviour that is essential to the iDT4GDC hardware optimisation engine (KER-4).

BitNet's hands-on experience with real-world data centre hardware deployment ensures that the platform's optimisation algorithms are grounded in practical infrastructure realities. Their contribution to the Integrated Dashboard (KER-7) focuses on making complex operational data accessible through intuitive interactive visualisations.

Contributions to iDT4GDC
  • Primary contributor to WP4: Hardware Optimisation
  • Developing the Hardware Optimisation Engine
  • Hardware profiling, efficiency characterisation, and allocation algorithms
  • Interactive data visualisation components for KER-7 (Integrated Dashboard)
  • Heterogeneous hardware integration testing and validation
  • Contributing to KER-5 (Integrated System Demonstrator)
Location

🇹🇷 Turkey
Green Continuum
WP6 Lead Consortium Coordinator
Contact →

Green Continuum is a Turkey-based sustainability consultancy and technology company specialising in carbon benchmarking and digital sustainability solutions for the data centre industry. As the Work Package 6 leader, Green Continuum coordinates all exploitation activities, manages the consortium's Intellectual Property Rights strategy, and leads the commercial pathway for the iDT4GDC platform.

With deep expertise in the EU Green Deal regulatory landscape and the Climate-Neutral Data Centre Pact, Green Continuum ensures that iDT4GDC's technology aligns with current and emerging sustainability standards. The company brings an extensive industry network that enables effective dissemination to target markets.

Contributions to iDT4GDC
  • Leading WP6: Communication, Dissemination & Exploitation
  • Authoring and managing the Communication & Dissemination Plan
  • IPR strategy, patent landscape monitoring, and management reporting
  • Competence and SWOT analysis for market positioning
  • Exploitable Results documentation and commercialisation planning
  • Carbon benchmarking methodology in KER-7 (Integrated Dashboard)
  • Regulatory alignment with EU Green Deal and CNDCP
