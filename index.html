About the Project

Climate-Neutral Data Centre Technologies

iDT4GDC aims to develop Artificial Intelligence and Digital Twin technologies into a cloud AI platform to digitalise, optimise, and automate data centre operations for sustainability purposes.

iDT4GDC will guide data centre operations and management towards a sustainable future along five pillars of sustainability.

⚡
Power
AI-optimised power distribution and real-time efficiency monitoring
🌿
Carbon
Carbon Usage Effectiveness (CUE) optimisation beyond traditional PUE
💧
Water
Water Usage Effectiveness (WUE) management in cooling systems
♻️
Circular Economy
Hardware lifecycle optimisation and e-waste reduction strategies
🏛️
Governance
EU Green Deal compliance, reporting frameworks, and regulatory alignment
